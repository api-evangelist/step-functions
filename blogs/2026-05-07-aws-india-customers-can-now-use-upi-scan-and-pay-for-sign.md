India customers can now use UPI (Unified Payments Interface) Scan and Pay to sign up for AWS or make payments to their invoices. UPI is a popular and convenient payment method in India, which facilitates instant bank-to-bank transfers between two parties through mobile phones with internet. The new Scan and Pay experience simplifies payments by allowing customers to scan a QR code displayed on the AWS Console using their UPI mobile app (such as Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm, or Amazon Pay), eliminating the need to manually enter a UPI ID.
