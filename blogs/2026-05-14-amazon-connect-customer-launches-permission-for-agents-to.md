Amazon Connect Customer now supports a permission that gives agents access to their own performance evaluations in the Connect UI, without exposing other agents' evaluations, so they can review feedback to improve their performance. With this permission, agents can search for contacts where they have received an evaluation, view their evaluations alongside call recordings and transcripts, and submit an acknowledgment after reviewing.
