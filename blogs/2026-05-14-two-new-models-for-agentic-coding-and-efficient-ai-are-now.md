Today, AWS announced the availability of GLM-5.1-FP8 and Phi-4-mini-instruct in Amazon SageMaker JumpStart, expanding the portfolio of foundation models available to AWS customers. These models from Z.ai and Microsoft bring advanced agentic capabilities and efficient inference to enterprise AI workloads on AWS infrastructure. These models address different enterprise AI challenges with specialized capabilities: GLM-5.1-FP8 excels at agentic software engineering with sustained multi-round optimization, handling repository-level code generation, terminal tasks, and complex debugging workflows…
