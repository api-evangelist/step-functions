AWS Transform brings assessment, migration, and modernization into a single AI-powered experience that guides enterprises through their full transformation journey. Today, AWS announces support for customer-owned Amazon S3 buckets, giving customers full control over where their transformation artifacts are stored and how they are secured. With this launch, you can configure your own S3 bucket, optionally encrypt artifacts with your own AWS KMS key, and manage access policies through your own AWS account.
