Today, AWS Marketplace announces the Agreements API, enabling you to procure AWS Marketplace products and manage agreements programmatically. With this launch, you can generate estimates, accept offers, track charges and entitlements, update purchase orders and manage agreements all within your existing tools and workflows. Combined with the Discovery API, the Agreements API provides an end-to-end procurement journey from product discovery to purchase. You can integrate these APIs into your procurement systems to build custom workflows and streamline operations across your organization.
