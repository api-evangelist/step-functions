AWS RTB Fabric now supports custom domains for real-time bidding transactions received through external links . This capability helps advertising technology (AdTech) companies preserve their public endpoints and use owned domains—without requiring their partners to update their endpoint configurations. Endpoints (like bid.company.com/path) for real-time bidding workloads are typically representative of established, long-term traffic contracts.
