Amazon Web Services announces general availability of Amazon EC2 M3 Ultra Mac instances, powered by the latest Mac Studio hardware. Amazon EC2 M3 Ultra Mac instances are the next-generation EC2 Mac instances, that enable Apple developers to migrate their most demanding build and test workloads onto AWS. These instances are ideal for building and testing applications for Apple platforms such as iOS, macOS, iPadOS, tvOS, watchOS, visionOS, and Safari.
